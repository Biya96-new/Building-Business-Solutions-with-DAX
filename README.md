# 📊 Building Business Solutions with DAX

> Solving real business scenarios using DAX in Power BI

## 📖 Overview

This repository documents my learning journey through the **DAX Through One Dataset** challenge by **Codebasics**.

Instead of learning DAX functions in isolation, each day focused on solving a real business problem using the same dataset.

Over the course of 15 days, I worked on business scenarios ranging from revenue and profit calculations to dynamic KPIs, rolling averages, and CEO dashboards.

The goal wasn't just to learn DAX syntax—it was to understand the business requirement first and then choose the right DAX approach to solve it.

## 💡 Key Takeaways

This project helped me move beyond learning individual DAX functions.

Some of my biggest learnings include:

- Understanding the business problem before writing DAX.
- Reusing measures instead of repeating calculations.
- Building interactive dashboards using disconnected tables.
- Applying time intelligence to reveal business trends.
- Designing dashboards that are both dynamic and user-friendly.
